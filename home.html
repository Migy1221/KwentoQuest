ABOUT

Kadiliman is a Filipino-Mythology-themed survival stealth horror mystery video game. The player is trapped in a nightmarish unknown dimension of a medical school and must find a way to escape before they get trapped in that dimension forever. While escaping, the player will also have the opportunity to uncover the truth as to why they were trapped in that dimension.

Characters

Maxine

Maxine is supposedly a medical student who currently studies at Wright-Ridley Medical School. However, she finds herself within decrepit hallways rather than a functioning medical facility. Driven by fear and curiosity, she must figure out the mystery behind Wright-Ridley in hopes of escaping its hellish grip.

Gameplay

In Kadiliman, you will face various challenges that require stealth, puzzle-solving, and strategic thinking. The gameplay is designed to immerse you in a world where every decision counts and danger lurks around every corner.
