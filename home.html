ABOUT

Kadiliman is a Filipino-Mythology-themed survival stealth horror mystery video game. The player is trapped in a nightmarish unknown dimension of a medical school and must find a way to escape before they get trapped in that dimension forever. While escaping, the player will also have the opportunity to uncover the truth as to why they were trapped in that dimension.

Characters

Maxine

Maxine is supposedly a medical student who currently studies at Wright-Ridley Medical School. However, she finds herself within decrepit hallways rather than a functioning medical facility. Driven by fear and curiosity, she must figure out the mystery behind Wright-Ridley in hopes of escaping its walls.

Tikbalang

This Tikbalang currently resides within the Infinite Library, shuffling between the rows of shelves. An unfortunate fellow, his transformation into an Aswang was a botched one, with parts of his body twisted and rotting off. He roams the library crying for help, only for those cries to be mistaken for haunting moans.

Tiyanak

Within the confines of the Morgue resides the Tiyanak, a devilish little creature. It seems to have been stillborn and has been resurrected recently, with no mother in sight. That doesn't mean it's not itching for.

Kapre

The Kapre is a tall, brooding figure who sits on a large tree located within the facility's courtyard. He does not talk much, unless annoyed or prompted to. Strangely enough, he doesn't seem to have a large cigar on hand like most kapres do.

Mananangal

The Manananggal is probably the most dangerous Aswang within the Wright-Ridley Medical School. She is sometimes seen circling above the Medical School Building, like vultures on a carcass. Rumor has it that no weary soul has ever survived an encounter with her, nor found her lower half without having died trying.
